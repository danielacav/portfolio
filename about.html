My Background, Approach & Experience

I am a professional industrial designer from Colombia, currently based in Ottawa, Canada. I have a keen eye for detail and a passion for creating meaningful and memorable experiences.
Through design, I have acquired the ability to perceive the world holistically. In addition to learning how to design for tangible and intangible outcomes, I became more attentive to emotions and driven to create purposeful products.
I enjoy experimenting and solving problems. As a team player, I am meticulous, sensitive, competitive, persistent, and focused on quality in everything I do.
