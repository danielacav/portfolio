What it is about

I created the visual language for the brand “Hachi”, a Colombian startup that offers products and services around the well-being of pets.
We created a moodboard that represented what the entrepreneurs wanted their clients to know, feel and get from their brand. From there, the graphic language and style for the colour palette, users, and core values were defined.
